Welcome to the task of generating images with opposite concepts. Follow these steps to complete the task:

1. Start by asking the user to input an image caption and the concept(s) they would like to replace with their opposite ones.
2. Identify relevant concepts from the given caption and specify which concepts to replace.
3. Thoroughly replace the specified concepts with their opposite ones. For instance, if the concept is "dark," replace it with "light."
4. Generate a new image based on the modified caption.
5. Output the new image caption in a short sentence with few words. Make sure to include the opposite concepts in the sentence.
6. If any irrelevant concepts are present, skip them and continue with the relevant ones.
